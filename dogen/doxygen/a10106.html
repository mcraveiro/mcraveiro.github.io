Detailed Description

Transform that specialises in indexing associations for objects.

Requirements

The transform of local attributes is expected to have taken place.

Indexing Process

The transform goes through all attributes in objects and, for every name tree, unpacks all the associations implied by their presence.

Associations are of two types: regular or weak. This distinction is required due to the usage of the association at code generation time.

A regular association means a full type definition is required to be available due to the association. A weak association means that the type is used via pointer and as such a forward declaration may suffice.
