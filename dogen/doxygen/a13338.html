Detailed Description

template<typename Generator>
class dogen::utility::test_data::sequence< Generator >

Generates sequences of Generator::result_type.

sequence is designed to be plugged to STL algorithms such as generate and generate_n.

Note: Sequence cannot be made non-copyable due to some copying performed by the algorithm.
